What is Endowment Insurance?

Endowment insurance combines life insurance protection with a savings component. It guarantees a lump sum payout either at policy maturity or upon death, whichever comes first.

This advanced calculator uses actuarial mathematics and real mortality data from the Social Security Administration to determine fair premium pricing based on your age, desired payout, and risk tolerance.
